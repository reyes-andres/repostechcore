Solutions

People Counter

People Counter runs fully embedded in standard ceiling mounted network cameras and automatically counts in real time the number of people passing under the camera and in what direction.
People Counter provides the critical data you need to leverage your business. Use precise knowledge about visitor traffic to:

  • Measure how many of your visitors that actually buy
  • Analyze customer flows and traffic trends
  • Evaluate impact of advertising and promotions
  • Improve staff planning
  • Determine optimal opening hours
  • Identify and reward high performing stores and employees

Parking Counter

The Parking Counter is a product for counting and managing occupancy in parking garages. The software installs directly into standard IP cameras, which are placed in the ceiling over entrances and exits for counting vehicles to and from the facility.
