Network Cabling - (Cat, 5e, Cat 6, Cat 6a, Cat 7)

A cabling system is only as good as its design and installation. It is the most important investment you will make in your IT infrastructure and will be part of your network that will last the longest. It is the foundation on which all other business activities depend. Each aspect is highly specialized and requires trained professionals to ensure performance and reliability.

With a repostech installed system you will get...

Most vendors can install network equipment and set up basic networks, but repostech provides the experience customers deserve. Repostech focuses not only on installing a network, but securing and fine-tuning that network to provide the highest level of security, stability and reliability.

  • SIEMON Certified Technicians - to insure quality and reliability the cable plant is installed in strict compliance to the TIA/EIA 568-B Standard.
  • Every cable is run through 12 rigid tests using a Fluke DTX-1800 tester. Test results are delivered to you upon the completion of every project.
  • The cable plant is certified and warranted for 25 years. This includes parts, labor and applications. This is backed by the manufacturer of the components and the Fugal Company, so your investment is secure.
  • Every cable, patch panel port and workstation jack is clearly labeled with durable, electronically printed labels for easy identification.
  • Each cable system comes with a set of Auto-Cad As-Built drawings clearly showing every workstation location with designated numbers, making it a helpful management tool

With a system designed and installed by repostech, complete with AutoCad As-Built Drawings and properly labeled cables, patch panels and face plates, the IT department completes, Moves, Adds and Changes in minutes instead of hours. This equals less downtime and more productivity
