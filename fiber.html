Fiber Optic Networking

repostech offers an array of Fiber Optic Networking solutions for both Local Area Networks (LAN) and Outside Plant (OSP) needs. We specialize in installing, connecting and maintaining fiber in new and current networks. All single/multi-mode installations are guaranteed to have the lowest possible loss and to meet or exceed all industry standards. Our ability to create a low-loss fiber system is achieved through keeping up with the latest connection/fusion splice technology. Our guarantee to achieve an optimal system is aided by testing each strand of fiber. Test results and labeling are a crucial component in our installed fiber optic networks. All test results are recorded, compiled and submitted upon project completion. Contact us if you have a new project, emergency, or have a fiber network that lacks any of the crucial components you expect.
